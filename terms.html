Terms & Conditions

1. Admission Policy

Admissions at The Innovators Schooling System are based on merit, suitability, and adherence to the application process. All prospective students must submit accurate and verifiable academic, personal, and medical documents. The school reserves the right to verify information provided, and any discrepancies may result in withdrawal of admission or cancellation of enrollment. Parents and guardians are encouraged to attend orientation sessions to understand the school culture, academic expectations, and code of conduct for students.

2. Fee Payment

All school fees must be paid according to the schedule provided during admission. Fees include tuition, activity charges, and any other applicable services. Late payments may incur additional charges, and the school reserves the right to restrict access to facilities or suspend enrollment until outstanding dues are cleared. Refunds are processed only as per the schoolâ€™s refund policy. Parents are responsible for ensuring timely payments and maintaining records of all transactions.

3. Attendance

Regular attendance is mandatory for all students to ensure continuous learning and participation in school activities. Absences must be communicated in advance where possible, and extended absence without prior notice may affect academic evaluation. The school monitors attendance closely, and students who fail to meet attendance requirements may be restricted from participating in examinations or extracurricular events. Attendance records are shared with parents periodically.

4. Code of Conduct

Students are expected to maintain exemplary behavior at all times, showing respect towards teachers, staff, peers, and school property. Bullying, harassment, or any form of misconduct will not be tolerated. The school has a structured disciplinary framework, and repeated violations may lead to warnings, suspension, or even expulsion in serious cases. Parents are encouraged to discuss the code of conduct with their children and reinforce positive behavior at home.

5. Safety and Security

The school is committed to providing a safe and secure learning environment. Safety protocols are in place for classrooms, laboratories, playgrounds, and transportation. Parents and students are expected to cooperate with security measures, follow safety guidelines, and report any incidents immediately. The school may conduct safety drills and regular audits to ensure adherence to national and international safety standards.

6. Academic Integrity

Students must uphold the highest standards of academic honesty. Cheating, plagiarism, or submission of false information will be treated as serious violations. Teachers and administrators may use monitoring tools and assessments to ensure integrity. Consequences may include re-examination, grade penalties, or disciplinary action depending on the severity. Parents are encouraged to guide their children on the importance of honesty and integrity in learning.

7. Dress Code

All students are required to follow the schoolâ€™s prescribed dress code for uniformity and discipline. Proper attire promotes a professional and respectful learning environment. Non-compliance may lead to warnings and restrictions on participation in school activities until corrected. Parents should ensure students wear appropriate clothing, including physical education uniforms for sports activities and events.

8. Use of Technology

Technology resources, including computers, internet access, and educational software, must be used responsibly. Students should not engage in activities that compromise security, privacy, or ethical standards. Unauthorized use of devices or digital platforms may result in temporary or permanent suspension of access. Parents are encouraged to monitor digital usage and discuss responsible online behavior with their children.

9. Library & Lab Usage

The school library and laboratories provide essential resources for academic and creative development. Students must respect the materials, maintain cleanliness, and follow all usage rules. Damaging or misusing resources may result in fines or restricted access. Librarians and lab supervisors are available to guide students, and parents are encouraged to support responsible use of facilities to maximize learning opportunities.

10. Extracurricular Activities

Participation in extracurricular activities is encouraged to foster holistic development. Students must follow instructions, maintain discipline, and respect fellow participants. Certain activities may have additional requirements or fees. The school reserves the right to withdraw students from activities if behavior or performance standards are not met. Parents should ensure children attend activities punctually and prepared.

11. Transportation

School transportation services are available as per schedule and route allocation. Students must adhere to safety rules while on buses, including proper seating, no distraction to drivers, and respectful behavior. Parents are responsible for timely drop-offs or pickups if using private transportation. Any changes in transportation requirements must be communicated to the administration in advance.

12. Health & Medical

Parents must provide complete and accurate health information during admission. The school may offer first-aid and basic medical assistance. In case of emergencies, parents will be contacted immediately. The school is not liable for serious medical conditions or treatments, and parents are encouraged to maintain health insurance coverage for their children. Immunizations and routine health checks may be required as per regulations.

13. Communication

Official communication from the school will be conducted via email, phone, newsletters, or the school portal. Parents and students are expected to check messages regularly to stay informed about schedules, events, and policy updates. Miscommunication due to negligence will not exempt parents or students from compliance. Clear and timely communication is encouraged to foster a cooperative school environment.

14. Privacy Policy

The Innovators Schooling System respects the privacy of students and parents. Personal and academic information will be kept confidential and shared only with authorized personnel or as legally required. Parents consent to the use of photographs, videos, or student achievements for school promotion and documentation purposes unless explicitly opted out. Measures are taken to secure data from unauthorized access or breaches.

15. Amendments

The school reserves the right to modify or update these Terms & Conditions at any time to reflect changes in policies, regulations, or operational needs. All updates will be communicated through official channels such as the school website, portal, or email. Parents and students are expected to stay informed and adhere to the latest terms. Continued enrollment and participation constitute acceptance of any amendments.

The Innovators Schooling System is a premier educational institution shaping future leaders through innovative learning, academic excellence, and holistic development.
