Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

The Innovators Schooling System is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, and staff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard personal information and how individuals can exercise their privacy rights.

2. Information Collection

We collect personal information such as names, contact details, academic records, health information, and other relevant data necessary for school operations. Information may be collected directly from students, parents, staff, or through our website, applications, and digital services.

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used for purposes including academic administration, communication, health and safety management, student development, reporting, and school improvement initiatives. We ensure all usage complies with privacy and security standards.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade personal information. Data may be shared with authorized school staff, third-party service providers, or government authorities only when required for operational, legal, or educational purposes. Confidentiality is maintained at all times.

5. Security of Information

We implement physical, technical, and administrative measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our staff is trained to handle information responsibly, and access is granted only on a need-to-know basis.

6. Cookies and Online Tracking

Our website may use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance user experience, monitor usage patterns, and improve services. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings. No personally identifiable information is collected without consent.

7. Student Information

Student records, including grades, attendance, health information, and performance data, are maintained securely and accessed only by authorized staff. Parents and guardians may request access to their childâ€™s records in accordance with school policies.

8. Communication

Official communications, notifications, and newsletters may contain personal information. Parents and students are encouraged to keep contact information up to date to receive timely information. We do not share communication data with third parties for marketing purposes.

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers for educational software, transportation, or health services. These providers are required to comply with privacy obligations and safeguard any personal information shared with them.

10. Consent

By enrolling with The Innovators Schooling System, parents and students consent to the collection and use of personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. Consent can be withdrawn in writing, subject to compliance with legal and operational requirements.

11. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill operational, legal, or regulatory purposes. Records may be archived or securely destroyed after the retention period ends.

12. Rights of Individuals

Students and parents have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information. Requests should be submitted to the school administration, and we will respond in accordance with applicable privacy laws and school policies.

13. Updates to Privacy Policy

The school may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in regulations, technology, or operational requirements. All updates will be communicated through official channels, including the school website, portal, or email notifications.

14. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, parents, students, or staff may contact the school administration via email, phone, or through the school portal. We are committed to resolving privacy-related inquiries promptly and transparently.
